## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does the test work?**
Complete 4 sections in order: Reading, Grammar, Vocabulary, and Listening. Each section has 15 questions. When you finish all 4 sections you will see your CEFR level result.

**What levels does the test evaluate?**
The test evaluates English levels from A1 (Beginner) to B2 (Upper-Intermediate) based on the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR).

**Is the app free?**
Yes, the app is completely free with no ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.

**Does the app work offline?**
Yes, the app works fully offline. No internet connection is required.

**How do I delete my test history?**
Uninstall and reinstall the app to clear all saved data.
