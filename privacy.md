
# Privacy Policy — English Level Test A1 B2

Last updated: March 10, 2026

## No Personal Data Collected

English Level Test A1 B2 does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

## What the app does

- All test results are stored locally on your device only
- No account or login is required
- No internet connection is required to use the app
- No analytics or tracking tools are used
- No third-party services receive any data

## Device Storage

The app saves your test history locally on your device using the browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app.
